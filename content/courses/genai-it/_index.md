**Free · Beginner · 5 Units · 20 Modules · No coding required**

AI/ML hiring on Naukri grew 45% in FY26 while overall IT hiring remained flat. Over 77,000 AI vacancies are live right now — and most of them do not require you to build AI. They require you to *work with* it effectively.

This free course teaches you exactly that. No maths, no jargon, no prior experience needed.

---

## What you will learn

By the end of this course you will be able to:

- Explain how Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) work — in plain language
- Write effective prompts using zero-shot, few-shot, and chain-of-thought techniques
- Use ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini strategically for your specific job role
- Build a personal prompt library tailored to your daily work tasks
- Understand RAG, AI agents, and automation workflows without deep technical knowledge
- Position your GenAI skills on your resume, LinkedIn, and Naukri profile
- Identify freelancing and career opportunities in the Indian AI market

---

## Who this course is for

This course is designed specifically for **Indian IT professionals and students** who want to use Generative AI at work — not build it from scratch.

You will benefit most if you are a software developer, QA engineer, business analyst, data analyst, project manager, IT manager, or final-year engineering student preparing for the job market.

**Prerequisites:** None. A smartphone or laptop and curiosity are enough.

## How the course works

This course is self-paced — read one module per day and you finish in 4 weeks. Each module takes 10–15 minutes to read. Every unit ends with one small, practical assignment that takes 20–30 minutes.

There are no video lectures, no sign-ups, and no paywalls. Everything is free and available immediately.

At the end of the course, you will have a prompt library, an AI workflow, and a completed AI Toolkit document — real deliverables you can reference at work and showcase to employers.

**Is this course really free?**  
Yes, completely free. No registration, no payment, no hidden fees.

**Do I need coding experience?**  
No. This course is designed for all IT professionals, including non-developers. Unit 3 has a dedicated developer module if you do code, but it is not required.

At one module per day, approximately 3–4 weeks. Each module is a 10–15 minute read. You can go faster or slower — the content is always available.

**Which AI tools do I need?**  
A free ChatGPT account is enough to complete all assignments. Claude and Gemini both have free tiers and are recommended for comparison in Unit 1. No paid subscriptions are required.

**Will I get a certificate?**  
A completion certificate is in development. Once available, it will be free and shareable on LinkedIn and Naukri. Subscribe to [chandrashaker.in](https://chandrashaker.in) to be notified.

**Is this relevant to my job at TCS / Infosys / Wipro / HCL?**  
Yes — Unit 3 is built around real Indian IT workflows. The prompt library assignment in Unit 3.4 is specifically designed to be immediately useful in your current role.
